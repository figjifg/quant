# Broker Research Worksheet

Status: USER HOST COLLECTION PENDING.

This worksheet is for user host collection only. Codex must not fill broker
fees, FX spreads, product lineups, or tax-report claims from memory or external
network access. Use official broker homepages, terms, fee notices, product
lineup pages, and customer-service confirmations where needed. Recheck all
items immediately before live deployment.

## Collection Sources

Preferred sources:
- Broker official homepage
- Official fee and commission notice
- Official overseas stock/ETF trading guide
- Official FX/exchange-rate spread guide
- ISA, pension-savings, and IRP product lineup pages
- Official tax-report or overseas-stock tax filing guide
- Official order-type guide or HTS/MTS manual

Avoid:
- Blogs
- Community comments
- Old screenshots
- Promotion summaries without expiry dates

## Collection Procedure

For each broker:

1. Visit the broker official homepage.
2. Search the site for `해외주식 수수료 안내` or equivalent.
3. Record overseas ETF commission, minimum commission, promotion terms, and
   promotion expiry.
4. Search for `환전 우대`, `외화환전`, or equivalent.
5. Record normal FX spread, preferential/event spread, eligibility condition,
   and expiry.
6. Confirm whether automatic FX conversion is supported for overseas ETF orders.
7. Check tax-report pages for capital gains, dividends, lot history, HIFO or
   lot-selection handling, and KRW 2.5 million exemption support.
8. Check ISA, pension-savings, and IRP pages for domestic-listed US equity and
   Treasury ETF substitutes.
9. Check whether fractional US ETF trading is available for SPY, QQQ, IEF, or
   relevant substitutes.
10. Check order functions such as VWAP, scheduled orders, recurring orders, or
    reservation orders.
11. Fill this worksheet and update `docs/t007_broker_comparison.md`.
12. Immediately before live deployment, repeat the checks and update the
    collection date.
