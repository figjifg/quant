# K003 K-family Validation

Status: BACKLOG (after K002)

## Purpose

Pre-register K-family promotion rules before any sector candidate can be
considered beyond the backlog candidate library.

## Promotion Criteria

A candidate must be clearly better than `P08_IEF30`:

- Better return, max drawdown, and Sharpe.
- Improves 2022, GFC, COVID, and 2025 spike stress windows.
- Remains superior after cost and tax.
- Wins across subperiods.
- Passes long-history stress.

## Failure Rule

If any criterion is not met, the candidate remains a backlog library entry,
matching the N-family treatment.

K-family is a US sector sleeve diagnostic, not a primary allocation family and
not authorization for live rotation.
